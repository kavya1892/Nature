About

Nature content encompasses the natural world, including living organisms, landforms, weather, and celestial bodies. It also includes the physical processes and interactions that occur within the natural world. In essence, nature is the world as it exists without human intervention, encompassing everything from the smallest microbe to the largest galaxy.

Nature is all the animals, plants, and other things in the world that are not made by people, and all the events and processes that are not caused by people.
